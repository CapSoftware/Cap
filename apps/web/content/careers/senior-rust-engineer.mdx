We're looking for a Senior Rust Engineer to join our team and help build the future of screen recording software.

## About the Role

As a Senior Rust Engineer at Cap, you'll be working on our core desktop application, built with Tauri and Rust. You'll be responsible for:

- Building and maintaining our core recording engine
- Optimizing performance and reducing resource usage
- Implementing new features and improvements
- Collaborating with the team on architecture decisions

## Requirements

- 3+ years of experience with Rust
- Experience with desktop application development
- Strong understanding of performance optimization
- Experience with FFmpeg or similar multimedia frameworks
- Familiarity with Tauri is a plus

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity
- Flexible working hours
- Remote-first culture
- Health insurance
- Learning and development budget
