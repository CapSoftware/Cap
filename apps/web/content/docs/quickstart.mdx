This guide will take you from zero to a shared recording in under a minute. By the end, you will have recorded your screen, gotten a shareable link, and sent it to someone.

## Step 1: Install Cap Desktop

If you have not already, download and install Cap Desktop from [Cap.so/download](https://cap.so/download).

For detailed installation instructions including platform-specific permissions, see the [Installation guide](/docs/installation).

## Step 2: Sign In

Open Cap Desktop and sign in with your Cap account. If you do not have one yet, you can create a free account directly from the app or at [Cap.so](https://cap.so).

Signing in connects your desktop app to the Cap cloud so your recordings can be shared instantly.

## Step 3: Configure Your Recording

Before you hit record, take a moment to set up your capture:

1. **Choose what to record** - Select your entire screen, a specific window, or a specific application. Click the screen selector dropdown to choose.

2. **Camera (optional)** - Toggle the camera button to include a webcam overlay in your recording. Your face will appear as a floating circle in the corner of the video.

3. **Microphone (optional)** - Toggle the microphone button to capture audio narration along with your screen.

The first time you use the camera or microphone, your operating system will ask for permission. Click **Allow** to proceed.

## Step 4: Record

Click the **Record** button to start capturing. You will see a brief countdown before recording begins.

While recording:

- A small indicator shows that Cap is actively recording
- Continue using your computer normally
- Navigate through whatever you want to show

When you are done, click the **Stop** button in the Cap toolbar or use the keyboard shortcut:

- **macOS**: `Cmd + Shift + 2`
- **Windows**: `Ctrl + Shift + 2`

## Step 5: Share

After you stop recording, Cap processes your video and generates a shareable link that is ready to share.

Copy and paste the link anywhere:

- Slack or Teams messages
- Email
- GitHub issues or pull requests
- Notion documents
- Anywhere you can paste a URL

The recipient clicks the link and watches your recording instantly in their browser. No downloads, no accounts required.

## What Happens Next

Your recording is now available on your [Cap dashboard](https://cap.so/dashboard) where you can:

- **Rename** your recording with a descriptive title
- **Protect** the share link with a password
- **View analytics** to see who watched and for how long
- **Download** the original video file
- **Delete** the recording when you no longer need it

## Tips for Great Recordings

- **Keep it short.** Recordings under 3 minutes tend to get watched all the way through.
- **Narrate as you go.** Turn on your microphone and talk through what you are doing. Audio makes recordings much more useful.
- **Show your face.** A camera overlay adds a personal touch and keeps viewers engaged.
- **Clean up your screen.** Close unnecessary tabs and notifications before recording.
- **Use a descriptive title.** After recording, give it a clear name on your dashboard so viewers know what to expect.
