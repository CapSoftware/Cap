Instant Mode is the fastest way to capture and share your screen with Cap. Record your screen, stop, and get a shareable link quickly. There is no file management and no extra steps. Once processing completes, your link is ready.

## How Instant Mode Works

Instant Mode quickly uploads your recording to the Cap cloud (or your self-hosted instance) when you stop. This means:

- **Quick upload processing** after you stop recording
- A **shareable link is generated quickly** after processing
- The link is **ready to copy and share**
- Viewers can start watching immediately

This makes Instant Mode ideal for quick walkthroughs, bug reports, async standups, and any situation where speed matters.

## Starting a Recording

### From the App

1. Open Cap Desktop.
2. Make sure **Instant Mode** is selected (this is the default).
3. Choose what you want to capture using the screen selector.
4. Optionally enable your camera and microphone.
5. Click the **Record** button.

### Using a Keyboard Shortcut

You can start recording without even opening the Cap window:

- **macOS**: `Cmd + Shift + 2`
- **Windows**: `Ctrl + Shift + 2`

This uses your last recording configuration (screen selection, camera, microphone settings).

## Choosing What to Record

Before recording, select your capture source:

**Full Screen** - Captures everything on your selected display. Best for demos that span multiple windows.

**Specific Window** - Captures only one application window. The recording will follow that window even if you resize or move it. Best for focused tutorials.

## Camera Overlay

Toggle the camera button to include a webcam overlay in your recording. The camera overlay appears as a floating circle that shows your face while you present.

You can:

- Choose which camera to use if you have multiple connected
- Position the overlay in any corner of the recording
- Resize the overlay

See [Camera & Microphone](/docs/recording/camera-and-mic) for detailed configuration options.

## Microphone Audio

Toggle the microphone button to narrate your recording. Audio narration makes recordings significantly more useful and engaging.

You can:

- Choose which microphone to use
- Monitor audio levels before recording

See [Camera & Microphone](/docs/recording/camera-and-mic) for audio settings and troubleshooting.

## During Recording

While recording, you will see a recording indicator. Continue using your computer normally. Everything on the captured screen or window will be recorded.

**To pause**: Use the pause keyboard shortcut to temporarily pause recording. This is useful if you need to handle something private before continuing.

**To stop**: Click the **Stop** button in the Cap toolbar, or press the stop keyboard shortcut:

- **macOS**: `Cmd + Shift + 2`
- **Windows**: `Ctrl + Shift + 2`

## After Recording

The moment you stop recording:

1. Cap finishes processing the video in the background.
2. A **shareable link** is generated and ready to share.
3. You will see a notification confirming the link is ready.
4. Copy the link and paste it anywhere to share it.

The recording also appears on your [Cap dashboard](https://cap.so/dashboard) where you can rename it, adjust share settings, view analytics, or delete it.

## Sharing Your Recording

The shareable link takes viewers to a dedicated page where they can:

- Watch the recording in a high-quality video player
- Adjust playback speed
- Read AI-generated captions
- Leave timestamped comments
- Download the video (if enabled)

For more on share settings, see [Share a Cap](/docs/sharing/share-a-cap).

## When to Use Instant Mode

Instant Mode is perfect for:

- **Bug reports** - Show the exact steps to reproduce an issue and paste the link in your issue tracker.
- **Quick tutorials** - Walk someone through a process step by step.
- **Async standups** - Record a quick update for your team instead of writing it out.
- **Code reviews** - Walk through your changes visually.
- **Customer support** - Show a customer how to do something in your product.
- **Feedback** - Record your screen while reviewing a design, document, or pull request.

## Instant Mode vs Studio Mode

| Feature | Instant Mode | Studio Mode |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Upload | Automatic after stopping | Manual, saves locally first |
| Share link | Available quickly after processing | Available after editing and uploading |
| Editing | None | Full editing suite |
| Background customization | No | Yes |
| Zoom effects | No | Yes |
| Trimming | No | Yes |
| Best for | Quick communication | Polished, professional content |

If you need editing tools, background customization, or zoom effects, use [Studio Mode](/docs/recording/studio-mode) instead. For everything else, Instant Mode gets you a shareable link in the least amount of time.
