- **Studio Mode overhaul** - Movement, motion blur, and styling improvements, including new and improved cursors, motion blur for zooms and fast movement, smoother auto-zoom, squircle corners, and more.
- **Microphone channel downmixing** - Microphone audio frames are now downmixed for better consistency across devices.
- **Auto-recover capture failures** - Automatically recovers desktop microphone feed and ScreenCaptureKit stream failures on macOS.
- **macOS capture reliability** - Simplified system stop error checks in screen capture to reduce spurious failures.
